## рҹ“– Overview

Bloom is a frontend wellness application designed to make self-care feel simple, personal, and encouraging.

Instead of overwhelming users with statistics and complicated dashboards, Bloom introduces a friendly companion that guides users through a short daily wellness check-in. Their responses are saved locally, allowing them to view their daily summary and track their wellness journey over time.

Bloom was built with the idea that small daily habits can lead to meaningful long-term improvements.
