# 🌱 Bloom — Student Wellness Companion

> A calming, companion-based wellness application that encourages students to build healthy daily habits through personalized check-ins and thoughtful reflections.

---

## 📖 Overview

Bloom is a frontend wellness application designed to make self-care feel simple, personal, and encouraging.

Instead of overwhelming users with statistics and complicated dashboards, Bloom introduces a friendly companion that guides users through a short daily wellness check-in. Their responses are saved locally, allowing them to view their daily summary and track their wellness journey over time.

Bloom was built with the idea that small daily habits can lead to meaningful long-term improvements.

## 💡 How It Works

1. Welcome to Bloom
2. Enter your name
3. Name your wellness companion
4. Meet your companion
5. Complete your daily wellness check-in
6. Receive a personalized wellness message
7. View your daily summary
8. Track previous wellness check-ins

---

## 🌱 Why Bloom?

Many wellness applications focus heavily on numbers, streaks, and productivity.

Bloom takes a different approach by creating a supportive companion that encourages consistency through small daily habits. The goal is to make wellness feel welcoming instead of overwhelming.
